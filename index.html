Projects

Fantastic Funding

A platform for tech creators to sell their products in a fair and eqitable way.

Technologies: Ruby On Rails, Postgresql, Vue, Nuxt, AWS

Fantastic Funding is a kickstarter-like platform for tech creators to sell their products to their loyal fans. The platform is build on a Ruby on Rails API which feeds into a Nuxt.js frontend leveraging Server Side Rendering for SEO purposes. Behind the scenes we integrated a Stripe payment solution for pledges and paying out creators.

DuanDuanZu

Short-term commerical space rental based in China.

Technologies: Django, Postgresql, Redis, Vue, WeChat Mini Program, AWS

A platform to post your underutilized commerical space and users to find the perfect location to do their work. DuanDuanZu was a facilitating platform to link these two parties together in China. The platform was build on a Django REST Framework backend with Postgresql and Redis for database and key store services. On the frontend, we used Vue and WePY (a Vue-like WeChat Mini Program framework) to deliver a high-quality experience to space owners and users alike.
